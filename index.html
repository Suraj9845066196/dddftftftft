Martial Arts We Teach

These are the main martial arts delivered in our weekly timetable: Jiu-jitsu, Karate, Judo and Muay Thai.

Jiu-jitsu

Focuses on grappling, control and technique. I like this class for confidence building and real self-defence skills.

  • Beginner friendly
  • Technique + control
  • Suitable for teens/adults

Karate

Great for discipline and striking basics. This class is structured and helps improve focus and fitness.

  • Discipline + forms
  • Striking fundamentals
  • Good for all levels

Judo

This training is based on throws, balance and movement. It improves coordination and body control.

  • Throws + balance
  • Safe technique learning
  • Strong cardio session

Muay Thai

High-energy kickboxing style training. It is perfect for endurance, strength and full-body conditioning.

  • Strikes + conditioning
  • Great calorie burn
  • Improves stamina

Note: The gym has a large matted martial arts area, a fully equipped gym, and wellness facilities (sauna and steam room), with changing and shower facilities.

Kids Classes

Why kids training matters
